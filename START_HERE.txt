========================================
  RAJUHERO ZIP — HOW TO OPEN
========================================

1) Unzip rajuhero.zip
2) Open the footbaaal folder


EASIEST WAY (no localhost needed)
---------------------------------
Double-click:  OPEN-WEBSITE.bat   (Windows)
or double-click:  index.html


LOCALHOST WAY (Windows)
-----------------------
Double-click:  START-WINDOWS.bat

It will open:
  http://localhost:3000

Keep the black window open.
To stop: press Ctrl+C or close the window.


LOCALHOST WAY (Mac)
-------------------
Double-click:  START-MAC.command
Then open: http://localhost:3000


ADMIN
-----

IF LOCALHOST STILL FAILS
------------------------
Use OPEN-WEBSITE.bat or index.html instead.
That opens the full website without localhost.
